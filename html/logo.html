Goggl is #Girlboss Approved

Goggl (pronounced gah-gul) is a simple, easy-to-use productivity organizer platform.

Designed for Girlbosses on the Go, Goggl organizes completed tasks in an easy-on-the-eyes Pink theme.

For extra motivation, return to ANGRY GIRLBOSS (pictured below), an easy reminder of the Girlbosses you let down with each interruption.

To add your own task, simply navigate to Add Task in the navigation bar.
